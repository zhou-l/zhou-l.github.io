Dr Liang Zhou is an assistant professor at the National Insitute of Health Data Science, Peking University. His reserach interest include scientific and information visualization, visual analysis of health data, and medical mixed reality. 

He received a Ph.D. in Computing at the University of Utah. Prior to his current position, he worked at the Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute (SCI Institute), University of Utah and the Visualization Research Center (VISUS), University of Stuttgart.
